Twitter sentiment analysis

Free and Open-sourced

SmmMobile is a Real-Time, Twitter Sentiment Analyzer tool, based on Naive Bayes classifier as demonstrated on smm.streamcrab.com

It tries to determine the attitude (Positve or Negative) of writer with respect to some topic or the overall tonality of a document

How it works

Every Tweet is analyzed for presence or absence of particular word/combination of words against training Dataset, that gives as a probability that a given text is positive or negative. Then all the probabilities combined together in order to calculate the overal tonality of Twitter chatter with respect to given topic or subject

Limitations

- Each query is limited to 50 pulls, thats about 5000 Tweets.
- Each IP is limited to 200 pulls / hour

If you need more than above specified limits, consider deploying your own Classifier Server or contact me
